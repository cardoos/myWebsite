I went with a website reflecting what makes her the best in her field, and a resume is the best way to display your skills. The website is optimized to run well on mobile devices as well as desktops. Regardless of what I build, responsive web-design takes priority. Just before Keys 4 cars became a client of mine they had recently had another company build a website for them. The result was a very pretty very bloated website. It ran fantastik on a desktop or laptop but struggled to load on mobile devices. Although it was not ideal, I was able to work with their newly built website to save them the cost of building another website. I first focused on getting the website optimized to run well on mobile devices, responsive web-design is much more than just looking good on any size screen, it needs to function and run well on devices with slow data connections. I found that the website was bloated due to the amount of additional plugins and unnecessary features that had been added to make the website look flashier than needed. With some changes to the backend of the existing website I was able to significantly speed up the Keys 4 Cars website and optimize the website for mobile devices.

MGIBOSTON.COM

Marble and Granite Internation is a family owned marble and specialty stone installer. They reached out to me looking for a website that they could update and maintain themselves after it was completed. I decided that Wordpress would be the best choice to fulfill their website needs. After deciding on a design, I developed the website and handed it off to them in late 2018 and they still maintain and update it on their own. Wordpress is one of the easiest website content management systems around and if you are looking for an easy to use, easy to update small business website then Wordpress websites are the way to go. They often get a bad rap for being bloated and slow or using antiquated technology. With a few tweaks and the right plugins a small business website built with Wordpress can be fast and efficient.
